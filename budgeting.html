Budgeting

Budgeting:

1. Track your expenses & income for one month 2. Create the categories that fit your life 3. Set some short-term & long-term financial goals 4. Cut certain areas to make those goals possible 5. Adjust your budget accordingly over time

Difference between wants and needs

It is important to start budgeting in order to learn the value of things. Some items are important and are a need for your family, owever, sometimes you can have extra money left over for fun things! You need to budget your money in order to save up for it :)
